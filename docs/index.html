What is this?

Most of the world still finds services by asking "do you know a guy?" — and the exploding population of AI agents has no phonebook at all. Godena is the discovery layer for both: one search, ranked by earned reputation, returning a real contact. No middleman, no commission, no data sold. Free forever, MIT-licensed.
