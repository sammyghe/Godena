# Godena — Roadmap

Godena grows along a five-stage path. Each stage unlocks the next; we do not skip ahead.

```
Stage 1   Directory          ← we are here
Stage 2   Smart matching
Stage 3   Reputation network
Stage 4   Transactions
Stage 5   Ecosystem
```

- **Stage 1 — Directory.** A searchable registry of agents and services, reachable over WhatsApp. Working today.
- **Stage 2 — Smart matching.** Better understanding of messy, natural requests ("affordable lawyer for a land case near Ntinda"), not just keywords.
- **Stage 3 — Reputation network.** Trust earned through real jobs and ratings becomes the primary ranking signal. This is the moat.
- **Stage 4 — Transactions.** Optional booking, payment, and escrow on top of trusted connections — without becoming a forced middleman.
- **Stage 5 — Ecosystem.** Federated city networks, agent-to-agent discovery, and an open standard others build on.

## The engine: add → check → rate

Every healthy listing moves through a loop. Stage 1 has **add**; the work now is to close **check** and **rate**, because those are what turn a static list into a living, trusted network.

- **ADD** — new agents enter continuously: self-registration over WhatsApp, the public API, scheduled seeding from open sources (e.g. OpenStreetMap businesses, public AI-agent registries), and community pull requests. Idempotent — duplicates are skipped.
- **CHECK** — listings are verified and kept honest: is the contact reachable, does the link resolve, is a named brand's number actually official, is the entry claimed? Stale or unreachable entries are demoted or hidden. **A dead contact is worse than an empty result.**
- **RATE** — after an introduction, the person is asked one light question — *"how did it go?"* — and the answer feeds the reputation score. This single return-step is what converts identity into earned trust.

## Milestones

These are the public checkpoints. We treat them as gates, not vibes.

1. **Liquidity that holds.** The most common searches across launch cities each return three real, reachable results — quality of the top results, not raw row count.
2. **The loop closes.** The post-introduction rating step is live and a full add → find → contact → rate cycle runs end to end.
3. **Trust is visible.** Verified and claimed providers are clearly distinguished from unverified ones; no fabricated data is ever shown as real.
4. **Open and forkable.** Clear license, contribution path, and security policy so anyone can run a city network or build on the API.
